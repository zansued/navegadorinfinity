PUT YOUR LOGO HERE — 3 PNG FILES
=================================
Chrome needs your logo in 3 sizes. Create these from your 500x500 ToolzBuy logo:

  icon16.png   — 16 x 16 pixels
  icon48.png   — 48 x 48 pixels
  icon128.png  — 128 x 128 pixels

All three go in this "icons" folder, named EXACTLY as above (lowercase).

HOW TO MAKE THEM from your 500x500 logo:
- Easiest: go to https://www.iloveimg.com/resize-image
  Upload your logo -> resize to 16x16 -> download -> rename to icon16.png
  Repeat for 48 and 128.
- Or any image editor (Paint, Photoshop, Canva): resize + export as PNG at each size.
- Keep them SQUARE and PNG with transparent background if possible.

After adding all 3 files, reload the extension (chrome://extensions ->
remove -> Load unpacked). Your logo will replace the grey "T".
